Services

  • Accounts

    Self Assesment   more

    We offer all services relevant to self assessment. These include: Preparation of individual and corporate self assessment tax returns, either for self employed, employees or company directors.
    Ensuring all self assessment tax returns are filed and submitted on time to the Inland Revenue.
    Calculation of all clients tax liabilities and advising them in advance so they are well prepared!
    Under the self assessment system for taxation, all tax payers are open to investigation by the Revenue and Customs on a random basis. We can advise you of the format of these investigations and guide you through them.


    VAT  more

    We can maintain your business records on a regular basis and prepare your VAT Returns to comply with HM Revenue & Customs legislation. Most
    HM Revenue & Customs inspections can be carried out at our premises.
    In addition, we can prepare periodical management accounts to enable you to monitor your business performance and to quickly assess any areas of your business which may require attention.


    HMRC Investigations  more

    For those unlucky enough to find themselves subject to HM Revenue & Customs Enquiries, we can assist in dealing with the points raised and help keep any penalties to a minimum.


    New Business Advice   more

    We offer business start up advice to include HM Revenue & Customs registration, business valuation, taxation planning, banking contacts and other areas of expertise.

  • Business


    Accounts  more

    We provide core accountancy work including preparation and finalisation of statutory accounts.


    Payroll  more

    We offer a comprehensive payroll service producing weekly/monthly wage slips and dealing with SMP, SSP, SAP, OSPP, ASPP and collection of student loans.
    Monthly remittance payslips for the Inland Revenue are automatically produced as are the end of year employer and employee forms P35 and P14. We can also assist with CIS requirements.


    Book Keeping  more

    It is essential that all businesses, large and small, keep accurate records for taxation and VAT purposes. We provide a complete book keeping service that includes returning your source documents to you fully referenced to aid the future tracing of documents and to maintain a sound audit trail. We ensure that every business detail is recorded accurately and accountably. Above all, we give you the peace of mind that your bookkeeping obligations have been met in a timely manner.


    Taxation Services  more

    The Taxes Acts place strict obligations on individuals and businesses to submit the required documents according to deadlines. We can provide peace of mind, by ensuring your returns are completed and filed on timely basis.

    Our Taxation compliance services include:
    • Self Assessment tax returns - Personal
    • Self Assessment tax returns - Partnership
    • Corporation Tax self assessment tax returns
    • Business Tax computations
    • Maintaining Payroll records and associated returns
    • PAYE Settlement Agreements
    • Construction Industry Scheme
    • Employment Related Securities returns
    • HM Revenue and Customs investigations preparation
    • PAYE and National Insurance Compliance

    Corporation Tax Services  more

    Corporation Tax is a tax on the profits of limited companies and other "corporate bodies" and can affect other organisations including clubs, societies, associations and charities.

    We offer a comprehensive service in this area and can support you by:-

    1. From your accounts and other records prepare your Corporation (CT) return and advise of your tax liability
    2. Assess your possible liability for tax on capital gains
    3. Advise you on your responsibilities and what you have to do
    4. Tell you when you have to do it and the time scale of compliance
    5. File your Company Tax returns online
    6. If there are major changes in the laws and regulations we will assess the impact on your Company and advise if appropriate
